Gulab Jamun

★★★☆☆ (3.8)

Gulab Jamun is a beloved Indian dessert made of soft, deep-fried dough balls traditionally crafted from khoya (milk solids), soaked in fragrant sugar syrup flavored with rose water, saffron, or cardamom. These golden orbs are tender, syrupy, and melt-in-the-mouth—often enjoyed warm or chilled after festive meals or celebrations. A true sweet indulgence with royal origins!
