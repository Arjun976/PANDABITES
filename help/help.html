Need Help?

We're here to make your food delivery experience seamless!

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign Up" on the homepage, enter your details, and verify your email. That’s it!

How do I change my delivery address?

Go to your profile settings and tap "Edit Address". You can add multiple addresses too.

Can I schedule a delivery?

Yes! While ordering, you can choose "Schedule Delivery" and set your preferred time slot.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery.

What if I get the wrong order?

You can contact support through the in-app chat or call us directly from your order history.
